Acts & Scenes
Structure your scenario. Each act groups related scenes. Each scene is one moment — one thing the character says, one decision the learner makes.
Tip: 3 acts with 3 scenes each is the sweet spot. Act 1 builds context, Act 2 raises the stakes, Act 3 tests for real skill.
